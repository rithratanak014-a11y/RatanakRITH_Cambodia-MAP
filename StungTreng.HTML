About

Stung Treng is a remote province in northeastern Cambodia along the Mekong and Sekong rivers. It is one of the least populated provinces in the country, known for pristine nature, rare Irrawaddy dolphins, and its border with Laos. The province is a hidden gem for eco-tourism and river adventures.

What it is known for
  • 🐬 Irrawaddy Dolphins
  • 🌊 Mekong & Sekong Rivers
  • 🌿 Eco-Tourism
  • 🇱🇦 Laos Border
