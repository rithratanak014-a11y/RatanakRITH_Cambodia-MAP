About

Takeo is a province in southern Cambodia, often called the "cradle of Khmer civilization" due to the many ancient pre-Angkorian temples found here. It borders Vietnam to the south and is known for its flat agricultural plains, rich history, and important archaeological sites.

What it is known for
  • 🛕 Phnom Chisor Temple
  • 🏊 Tonle Bati Lake & Temple
  • 🏺 Pre-Angkorian Heritage
  • 🌾 Rice Farming
📍 Top Places to Visit
🛕
Phnom Chisor
An 11th-century hilltop temple with panoramic views over the surrounding plains.
🏊
Tonle Bati
A popular lake and picnic area featuring ancient Angkorian temples on its shores.
🏺
Angkor Borei
An important pre-Angkorian archaeological site with artifacts tracing back thousands of years.
⛵️
Phnom Da
A hilltop temple accessible by boat, surrounded by flooded plains during rainy season.
🌾
Rice Fields
Endless green paddy fields that showcase Cambodia's agricultural heartland.
🏙
Takeo City Market
A busy local market offering fresh produce, street food, and everyday goods.
🌿
Rural Villages
Experience authentic Khmer village life along the peaceful countryside roads.
🐊
Crocodile Farm
A local farm where visitors can observe crocodiles and learn about wildlife conservation.
