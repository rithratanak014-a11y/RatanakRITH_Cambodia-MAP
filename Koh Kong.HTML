About

Koh Kong is Cambodia's most southwestern province, a vast wilderness of rainforests, mangroves, mountains, and coastline bordering Thailand and the Gulf of Thailand.It is home to the Cardamom Mountains — one of Southeast Asia's largest remaining rainforests — and Botum Sakor National Park, Cambodia's largest. With its Tatai River, pristine islands, and rich biodiversity, it is the country's premier eco-adventure destination.

What it is known for
  • 🌲 Cardamom Mountains Rainforest
  • 🚣 Tatai River & Mangroves
  • 🏝 Koh Kong Island
  • 🐘 Botum Sakor National Park
📍 Top Places to Visit
🌲
Cardamom Mountains
One of Southeast Asia's largest intact rainforests, home to Asian elephants, sun bears, and hundreds of bird species.
🚣
Tatai River
Kayak or boat through dense mangrove forests along this stunning river — a highlight for eco-tourists.
🏝
Koh Kong Island
Cambodia's largest island, 22 km long, with pristine beaches, clear waters, and excellent snorkeling.
🌊
Tatai Waterfall
A beautiful series of rapids and waterfalls on the Tatai River, ideal for swimming and relaxing in nature.
🦎
Botum Sakor National Park
Cambodia's largest national park, protecting coastline, forest, and rare wildlife including Irrawaddy dolphins.
🏕
Chi Phat Eco Village
A community-based eco-tourism village offering guided jungle treks and wildlife spotting in the Cardamoms.
