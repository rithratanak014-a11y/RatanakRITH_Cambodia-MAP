Kampong Thom

Khmer name: កំពង់ធំ
Capital: Kampong Thom
Population: 718,796

About

Kampong Thom is a large province in the heart of Cambodia, situated along the Stung Sen River. It is home to Sambor Prei Kuk — one of the most important pre-Angkorian temple complexes in Southeast Asia and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The province is rich in history, nature, and traditional Cambodian culture.

What it is known for
  • 🏯 Sambor Prei Kuk — UNESCO World Heritage
  • 🌊 Stung Sen River
  • 🌿 Dense Forests and Wildlife
  • 🏺 Pre-Angkorian History
📍 Top Places to Visit
🏯
Sambor Prei Kuk
A UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring over 100 pre-Angkorian temples dating back to the 7th century.
🌊
Stung Sen River
A scenic river running through the province — great for boat trips, fishing, and riverside relaxation.
🌿
Phnom Santuk
A sacred hill with Buddhist shrines, giant reclining Buddhas, and panoramic views of the countryside.
🏺
Prasat Andet
An ancient Khmer temple ruin surrounded by jungle, rarely visited and full of atmosphere.
🦋
Tmatboey Bird Sanctuary
A world-class birdwatching destination home to rare giant ibis and white-shouldered ibis species.
🌳
Kulen Promtep Wildlife Sanctuary
One of Cambodia's most important wildlife areas protecting rare birds, mammals, and forest ecosystems.
🛶
Floating Villages on Stung Sen
Visit communities living along the river in traditional stilt houses and floating homes.
⛩️
Wat Kampong Thom
The main provincial pagoda — a peaceful and ornate temple at the heart of the capital town.
🚴
Temple Cycling Tours
Rent a bike and explore the jungle-covered temple ruins and quiet rural roads around Sambor Prei Kuk.
🛍️
Kampong Thom Market
A lively local market in the town center selling fresh produce, street food, and handmade goods.
