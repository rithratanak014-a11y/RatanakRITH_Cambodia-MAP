Kampong Chhnang

Khmer name: កំពង់ឆ្នាំង
Capital: Kampong Chhnang
Population: 555,551
Area: 5,521 km²
About

Kampong Chhnang is a province in central Cambodia located along the Tonle Sap River. Its name means "Port of Pottery" in Khmer, reflecting its long tradition of clay pottery making. The province is famous for its floating villages, fishing communities, and scenic waterways connecting to the Tonle Sap Lake.

What it is known for
  • 🏺 Traditional Pottery Crafts
  • 🛶 Floating Villages
  • 🐟 Fishing Communities
  • 🌊 Tonle Sap River Access
