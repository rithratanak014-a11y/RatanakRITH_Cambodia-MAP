Khmer name: បាត់ដំបង
Capital: Battambang
Population: 1,252,982

About

Battambang is Cambodia's second largest city and one of the country's best-preserved French colonial towns. It is known for its vibrant arts scene, fertile rice paddies, and unique bamboo train experience.

What it is known for
  • 🚂 Bamboo Train (Norry)
  • 🏛️ French Colonial Architecture
  • 🌾 Rice Production
  • 🎪 Phare Ponleu Selpak Circus
📍 Top Places to Visit
🚂
Bamboo Train (Norry)
A iconic bamboo platform on rails — a unique and fun local transport experience.
🏯
Battambang Bat Caves
Watch millions of bats fly out of the cave at sunset — a breathtaking natural show.
🏛️
French Colonial Architecture
Stroll through streets lined with well-preserved French-era buildings.
🎪
Phare Ponleu Selpak Circus
A world-famous Cambodian circus school combining acrobatics, music, and storytelling.
🏰
Wat Banan
An ancient hilltop temple often called the "mini Angkor Wat" with panoramic views.
🏯
Wat Ek Phnom
An 11th-century Angkor-era temple surrounded by scenic rice fields.
🌾
Rice Fields
Cycle through vast, beautiful rice paddies — the agricultural heartland of Cambodia.
🎨
Sammaki Art Gallery
A local gallery showcasing contemporary Cambodian art and cultural works.
🛶
Sangker River Boat Trip
Take a peaceful boat ride along the river through local villages and landscapes.
🍊
Battambang Fruit Farms
Visit local farms famous for oranges, longans, and tropical fruits.
