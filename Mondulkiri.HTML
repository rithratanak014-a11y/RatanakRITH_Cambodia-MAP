About

Mondulkiri is Cambodia's largest and least densely populated province, located in the eastern highlands bordering Vietnam. Known as the "Wild East," it features rolling green hills, misty forests, stunning waterfalls, and is home to the Bunong indigenous people and their revered elephants. It is Cambodia's premier nature and wildlife destination.
