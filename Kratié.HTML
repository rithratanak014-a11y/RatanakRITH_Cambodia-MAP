Capital: Kratie Town
Population: 374,755

About

Kratie is a peaceful eastern province set along the banks of the Mekong River, renowned for being home to the critically endangered Irrawaddy freshwater dolphins.The charming riverside capital retains French colonial architecture and a relaxed atmosphere. Beyond the riverbanks, vast forests and rubber plantations define a largely untouched interior, making Kratie a favourite for eco-tourists and slow travellers.

What it is known for
  • 🐬 Irrawaddy Freshwater Dolphins
  • 🌊 Mekong River Scenery
  • 🏛 French Colonial Architecture
  • 🌴 Kampi Rapids & Sand Islands
📍 Top Places to Visit
🐬
Kampi Dolphin Watching
Take a traditional wooden boat 15 km north of Kratie town to spot the rare Irrawaddy dolphins.
🌊
Kampi Rapids
Beautiful river rapids and sandy islands — a stunning picnic spot during the dry season.
🏛
Phnom Sambok Pagoda
A hilltop Buddhist retreat 11 km from town with sweeping views of the Mekong and countryside.
🕌
Wat Sorsor Muoy Roy
The famous "100-pillar pagoda," an ancient temple built near a former 8th-century royal palace site.
🚴
Koh Trong Island
A tranquil river island accessible by ferry, perfect for cycling through villages and fruit orchards.
🌅
Kratie Riverside Promenade
A beautiful boulevard with food stalls, sunset views over the Mekong, and French-era buildings.
