About

Kampong Speu is a province in southern Cambodia located southwest of Phnom Penh. It is home to Phnom Aoral — the highest mountain in Cambodia — and is well known for its production of palm sugar, one of the country's most beloved traditional ingredients. The province offers scenic highlands, waterfalls, and a gateway to the Cardamom Mountains.

What it is known for
  • 🏔️ Phnom Aoral — Highest Peak in Cambodia
  • 🌴 Palm Sugar Production
  • 💧 Waterfalls and Nature
  • 🌿 Cardamom Mountain Gateway
📍 Top Places to Visit
🏔️
Phnom Aoral
The highest mountain in Cambodia at 1,813 metres — a challenging and rewarding trek through jungle trails.
💧
Kirirom National Park
A cool highland national park with pine forests, waterfalls, cliffs, and amazing views over the plains below.
🌴
Palm Sugar Farms
Visit local farms to see how traditional Cambodian palm sugar is harvested and made by hand.
🌊
Popok Waterfall
A beautiful hidden waterfall deep in the forest — a refreshing escape from the heat.
🌿
Cardamom Mountains
One of Southeast Asia's largest remaining rainforests, rich in wildlife, rivers, and trekking routes.
🏕️
Kirirom Hillside Resort Area
A popular weekend escape from Phnom Penh with cool weather, pine trees, and scenic hiking paths.
⛩️
Wat Kompong Speu
The main Buddhist temple in the provincial capital, a spiritual and cultural landmark for locals.
🐘
Wildlife Spotting
The forests of Kampong Speu are home to rare wildlife including elephants, leopards, and gibbons.
🚴
Rural Village Cycling
Cycle through quiet villages surrounded by palm trees and rice paddies for a peaceful local experience.
🛍️
Chbar Mon Market
The busy capital market of Kampong Speu offering fresh local produce, palm sugar products, and crafts.
