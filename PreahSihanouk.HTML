Preah Sihanouk

Khmer name: ព្រះសីហនុ
Capital: Sihanoukville (Kampong Som)
Population: 309,849
Area: 868 km²
About

Preah Sihanouk is Cambodia's premier coastal province, home to the country's most famous beaches and tropical islands. Located on the Gulf of Thailand, it features the bustling port city of Sihanoukville and stunning offshore islands including Koh Rong and Koh Rong Samloem. The province is a major hub for tourism, fishing, and international trade, with a deep-water port that handles much of Cambodia's cargo.

What it is known for
  • 🏖 Stunning beaches and coastline
  • 🏝 Koh Rong & Koh Rong Samloem islands
  • 🐠 Snorkelling and diving
  • ⚓ Sihanoukville deep-water port
📍 Top Places to Visit
🏝
Koh Rong
Cambodia's most famous island, known for its pristine white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant nightlife scene.
🌊
Koh Rong Samloem
A quieter, more laid-back island neighbour to Koh Rong with powdery beaches and bioluminescent plankton at night.
🏖
Otres Beach
A long, relaxed beach south of Sihanoukville city, popular with travellers seeking a quieter coastal atmosphere.
🐠
Snorkelling & Diving
The surrounding waters offer excellent snorkelling and scuba diving with colourful coral reefs and diverse marine life.
🌅
Sokha Beach
A sheltered, calm beach ideal for swimming, with soft sand and gentle waves perfect for families.
🦈
Ream National Park
A coastal national park with mangroves, dolphins, sea turtles, and some of Cambodia's most beautiful undeveloped beaches.
🚢
Sihanoukville Port
Cambodia's only deep-water seaport and a gateway for island-hopping boats heading to the Gulf of Thailand islands.
🌿
Koh Ta Kiev
A small, remote island with jungle camping, hammock bungalows, and some of the most untouched beaches in Cambodia.
