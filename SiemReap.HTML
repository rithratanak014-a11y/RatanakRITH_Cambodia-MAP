Siem Reap

Khmer name: សៀមរាប
Capital: Siem Reap
Population: 1,084,825
Area: 10,299 km²
About

Siem Reap is a province in northwestern Cambodia. It is home to the famous Angkor Wat temple complex, a UNESCO World Heritage Site visited by millions every year.

What it is known for
  • 🏯 Angkor Wat
  • 🏯 Bayon Temple
  • 🌳 Ta Prohm
  • 🌊 Tonle Sap Lake
