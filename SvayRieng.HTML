About

Svay Rieng is a province in southeastern Cambodia that borders Vietnam on three sides, giving it a unique "parrot beak" shape on the map. It is one of Cambodia's smaller provinces, known for its flat plains, strong agricultural output, and special economic zones that attract manufacturing investment.

What it is known for
  • 🦜 "Parrot's Beak" Geography
  • 🏭 Special Economic Zones
  • 🌾 Rice & Cassava Farming
  • 🇻🇳 Vietnam Border Trade
