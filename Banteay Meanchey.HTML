Banteay Meanchey

Khmer name: បន្ទាយមានជ័យ
Capital: Serei Saophoan
Population: 756,774
Area: 6,679 km²
About

Banteay Meanchey is a province in northwestern Cambodia bordering Thailand. Its name means "Fortress of Victory" in Khmer. The province is known for its busy border crossing at Poipet, ancient Khmer temples, and its role in Cambodia's trade and commerce with Thailand.

What it is known for
  • 🏰 Banteay Chhmar Temple
  • 🛃 Poipet Border Crossing
  • 🏯 Prasat Ta Prohm Bati
  • 🌾 Agriculture and border trade
📍 Top Places to Visit

Banteay Chhmar Temple
One of the largest and most remote Angkor-era temple complexes, famous for its intricate bas-reliefs.

A lively border town connecting Cambodia and Thailand, known for casinos and cross-border trade.
🏯
Prasat Yeay Laor
An ancient Khmer temple ruin surrounded by peaceful countryside and local villages.

The bustling provincial capital market offering local food, goods, and everyday Cambodian life.
