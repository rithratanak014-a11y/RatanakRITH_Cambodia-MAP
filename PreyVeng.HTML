About

Prey Veng is a densely populated province in southeastern Cambodia along the Mekong River. It is one of Cambodia's most important rice-growing regions and is known for its flat fertile plains, traditional Khmer culture, and strong agricultural heritage. The province has a rich history as a crossing point between the Mekong and Tonle Sap regions.

What it is known for
  • 🌾 Rice Production
  • 🌊 Mekong River
  • 🛕 Ancient Pagodas
  • 🌿 Fertile Floodplains
